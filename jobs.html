Cloud Engineer

At Overachieving Underachievers, anything is achievable

Position Reference: CE451

Location: Hawthorn, Melbourne

Salary: $100,000 - $110,000 per year

Reports to: Senior DevOps Manager

Quick Facts

  • Position Type: Full Time
  • Office: Advanced Technologies Centre
  • Work Mode: Hybrid
  • Start Date: ASAP

About the Role

We are looking for a motivated Cloud Engineer to join our infrastructure team. You’ll design and manage scalable, secure cloud-based environments across AWS and Azure, support DevOps pipelines, and contribute to automation initiatives.

About the Company

Overachieving Underachievers is a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. With clients in over 50 countries, we deliver solutions in cybersecurity, automation, cloud platforms, and digital transformation.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design, deploy, and maintain cloud infrastructure (AWS/Azure)
  2. Implement CI/CD pipelines and automation tools
  3. Ensure infrastructure compliance and security best practices
  4. Monitor system performance and troubleshoot incidents
  5. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate cloud systems

Reporting Structure

You will report directly to the Senior DevOps Manager and work alongside a small team of platform engineers, developers, and analysts.

Essential Qualifications and Skills

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 1–3 years of experience in cloud engineering (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  • Proficiency in scripting (Python, Bash, or PowerShell)
  • Understanding of networking protocols and cloud security
  • Experience with infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, CloudFormation)

Preferable Attributes

  • Experience with container orchestration (e.g., Kubernetes)
  • Certifications (AWS Certified Solutions Architect, AZ-104)
  • Familiarity with Linux administration and CI/CD systems
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Good communication and team collaboration abilities
